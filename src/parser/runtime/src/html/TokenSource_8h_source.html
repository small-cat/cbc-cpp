Errors from the lexer are never passed to the parser. Either you want to keep going or you do not upon token recognition error. If you do not want to continue lexing then you do not want to continue parsing. Just throw an exception not under RecognitionExceptionand Java will naturally toss you all the way out of the recognizers. If you want to continue lexing then you should not throw an exception to the parserâ€“it has already requested a token. Keep lexing until you get a valid one.
